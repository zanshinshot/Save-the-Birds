About Us

Mission Goal

Save The Bird is an organization that aims to bring awareness to the plight of birds all over the world. For centuries man has looked up to our winged friends as symbols of freedom and endless potential that has inspired our generation to reach for the skies. Thanks to their inspiration, we now reach for the stars and beyond, yet with our valiant innovations and advancements in technology, we hurt the environment our birds habitat in. We think that there is something wrong with that, and we wish to rectify this.
